Activities for Everyone

People at any experience level can get active! It doesnt matter if you are a complete beginer and have never been active before. Exercise doesnt have to be scary or be a big part of your day. It can start as simple as walking.

Create Your Fitness Routine

Not sure how to start? Try these simple ways to get moving:

  • Park a little further from where you are going
  • Take a 15-minute walk between classes
  • Try a workout challenge with friends
  • Make the most of your study breaks with quick exercises
  • Find easy 5 minute exercises online for a quick homework break

Want to get serious?

SLCC has a free gym for all students on campus. Try going with a friend or joining a class to learn the basics. If you dont want to do that try just going to ride a bike or walk on the treadmill

Rewards and Achievements

Setting goals and tracking your progress is a great way to stay motivated. Celebrate every milestone, no matter how small!
